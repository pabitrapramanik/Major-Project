About Jammu & Kashmir

Experience the crown jewel of India with breathtaking landscapes and rich heritage

Heaven on Earth

Nestled in the Himalayan mountains, Jammu & Kashmir is India's northernmost state offering unparalleled natural beauty. From the snow-capped peaks of Gulmarg to the tranquil waters of Dal Lake, this region promises unforgettable experiences.

Srinagar, the summer capital, is famous for its houseboats and Mughal gardens. The region boasts a unique cultural heritage influenced by Hindu, Buddhist, and Islamic traditions, reflected in its architecture, festivals, and way of life.

With its alpine meadows, pristine lakes, thrilling adventure sports, and warm hospitality, Jammu & Kashmir remains one of India's most sought-after tourist destinations.

Travel Tips

Essential information for planning your trip to Jammu & Kashmir

Best Time to Visit

April to October for pleasant weather and sightseeing. December to February for winter sports in Gulmarg. Ladakh is best visited June-September.

How to Reach

Major airports in Srinagar, Jammu, and Leh. Well-connected by road via NH44. Jammu Tawi is the main railway station. Domestic permits required for some areas.

Local Transportation

Taxi services available between major towns. Shikaras for lake transportation. Local buses and shared jeeps for inter-city travel.

Accommodation

Choose from luxury houseboats, heritage hotels, ski resorts, and budget guesthouses. Book in advance during peak seasons.
