About Delhi

Experience the capital city of India, where history meets contemporary charm

The Capital of India

Delhi, a city with a legacy spanning centuries, is a blend of ancient monuments, bustling markets, and modern skyscrapers. From Mughal-era forts to vibrant street food, it offers a dynamic travel experience.

The city is home to iconic landmarks like India Gate, Lotus Temple, and Qutub Minar, making it a must-visit for history lovers and cultural explorers.

Delhi's energy, rich heritage, and cosmopolitan lifestyle make it one of India's most fascinating destinations.

Culture & Heritage

Discover the diverse cultural essence of Delhi

🛍️

Markets & Shopping

Explore vibrant bazaars like Chandni Chowk and Connaught Place for traditional and modern shopping.

🎭

Festivals

Celebrate grand events like Republic Day Parade, Diwali, Holi, and Qutub Festival.

🍛

Cuisine

Enjoy Delhi’s famous street food like Chole Bhature, Paranthas, and the iconic Butter Chicken.

🎵

Music & Dance

Delhi's music and dance heritage includes the classical Kathak dance and soulful Qawwalis at Hazrat Nizamuddin Dargah. The city’s cultural scene reflects Mughal influences and a rich tradition of Sufi music.

Travel Tips

Essential information for your visit to Delhi

Best Time to Visit

October to March is ideal for sightseeing and outdoor activities.

How to Reach

Indira Gandhi International Airport is well-connected worldwide. Delhi is also a major railway hub.

Local Transportation

Use the Delhi Metro, buses, auto-rickshaws, and taxis for easy travel.

Accommodation

From luxury hotels in Connaught Place to budget stays in Paharganj, Delhi has options for all travelers.
